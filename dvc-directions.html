Directions to the DVC Campus

Planning to attend the Scion Exchange at Diablo Valley College in Pleasant Hill? Below are public transit routes, driving directions from across the Bay Area, and campus maps to help you find the Horticulture Building.

Public Transportation

BART + bus access to DVC campus.

🚉 Option A — Pleasant Hill BART

1Take BART to Pleasant Hill Station
2Transfer to Bus 316 toward DVC

🚉 Option B — Concord BART

1Take BART to Concord Station
2Take Bus 314 to Diablo Valley College

Driving Directions

Select the route closest to your starting point.

🧭 From Martinez & Points North

1Take I-680 South
2Exit 52 — Contra Costa Blvd
3Continue on Contra Costa Blvd to campus

🧭 From Hayward

1Take I-580 toward Oakland
2SR-13 → SR-24 → I-680 North
3Exit 51 — Willow Pass Rd
4Via Sunvalley Blvd → Contra Costa Blvd → Golf Club Rd → Old Quarry Rd to the Horticulture Building

🧭 From Marin County

1Cross the Richmond–San Rafael Bridge
2Richmond Pkwy → CA-4 East → I-680 South
3Exit 52, continue to Old Quarry Road

🧭 From San Francisco

1Bay Bridge via I-80 East
2Merge onto I-580, then CA-24 toward Walnut Creek
3Continue to I-680, Exit 51 — Willow Pass Rd
