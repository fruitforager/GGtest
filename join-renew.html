What We Do 🌳

  • Learn from guest speakers, workshops, and real-world experience
  • Tour gardens, orchards, & growing spaces
  • Trade scion wood, seeds, cuttings, and plants
  • Share tips, successes, and lessons learned
  • Taste rare & unusual fruit not found in markets

What You Get 🍊🍒 🥝

  • Orchard tours and hands-on workshops
  • Insider knowledge from experienced fruit growers
  • Seasonal harvests, tastings, and new varieties to discover
  • A local network for advice, troubleshooting, and inspiration
  • Scion Exchange early entry and plant sharing
  • Confidence to grow better fruit at home

Who Joins? 🌼

Growers from across the Golden Gate region — including Alameda, Contra Costa, Marin, San Francisco, San Mateo, Solano, and beyond. We even have members in Colorado and New York!

  • 🌱 ‎ New growers with no trees
  • 🏡 ‎ Backyard orchard enthusiasts expanding their plantings
  • 🌳 ‎ Small orchard and nursery growers sharing expertise
  • 🍒 ‎ Experienced fruit collectors and experimenters

Bring your curiosity. Come for the fruit. Stay for the community.
