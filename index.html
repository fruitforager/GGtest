Welcome to the Golden Gate Chapter. Our chapter is part of California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG.org), a nonprofit organization connecting fruit enthusiasts across California and beyond—with members in 48 U.S. states and over 35 countries. We're a community of amateurs and professionals dedicated to sharing the joy of rare fruit cultivation.

You don’t need to be an expert or even own a single tree to join us. Whether you’re growing on a balcony, in a backyard, or on acreage, there’s room for you here. No experience required — just curiosity, enthusiasm, and a love of growing.

What Happens at Meetings

Meetings typically include a speaker, a seasonal fruit tasting, the sharing of unusual plants, field trips, and the chance to connect with local growers.

Our signature event is the Golden Gate Scion Exchange — where you can collect cuttings to root, seeds, tubers, and rootstocks, learn grafting techniques, and get practical advice from experienced growers.

Everyone is Welcome 🌱

You don't need to be a member to attend — just show show up curious. Membership in CRFG is encouraged, and a Golden Gate Chapter membership is only $10/year.

Whether you’re a:

  • 🌱 ‎ New grower with no trees yet
  • 🏡 ‎ Backyard orchard enthusiast
  • 🌳 ‎ Small orchard or nursery grower
  • 🍒 ‎ Experienced collector or experimenter
