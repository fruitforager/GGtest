The Golden Gate Chapter of the California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG.org) is a vibrant community of professionals and amateurs dedicated to growing unusual fruits in the San Francisco Bay Area.

What Happens at Meetings 🌿

Our meetings include guest speakers, seasonal fruit tastings, orchard tours, and hands-on learning.
