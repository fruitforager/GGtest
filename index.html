The California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG) is a non-profit organization with chapters throughout California and members around the world. Our members — professionals and amateurs alike — are dedicated to growing, sharing, and learning about unusual fruits and edible plants.

The Golden Gate Chapter holds meetings across the San Francisco Bay Area featuring guest speakers, fruit tastings, shared potlucks, orchard tours, hands-on learning, and great conversation with people who share your passion for growing.

🍋 🍇 🌳 🍒 🥭

What Happens at Meetings 🌿

Meetings typically include a speaker, a seasonal fruit tasting, the sharing of unusual plants, field trips, and the chance to connect with local growers.

Our signature event is the Golden Gate Scion Exchange — where you can collect cuttings, seeds, tubers, and rootstocks, learn grafting techniques, and get practical advice from experienced growers.

Everyone Is Welcome‎ ‎ 🌱

You don't need to be a member to attend — just show up curious. Membership in CRFG is encouraged, and a Golden Gate Chapter subscription is only $10/year.
