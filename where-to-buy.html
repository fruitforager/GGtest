Where to Buy

Looking for rare fruit trees, berries, seeds, or edible plants? Here are nurseries and mail-order sources recommended by Golden Gate Chapter members.

Commercial Nurseries & Mail Order

  • 🍋
    Four Winds Citrus Mail-order specialists in citrus. Includes heat-to-ripen information by variety.
  • 🌱
    Berkeley Horticultural Nursery A beloved local retail nursery — one of the last with a deep expertise in how to handle bare root fruit trees.
  • 🌳
    Planting Justice One of the most biodiverse organic fruit tree nurseries in the country, a valuable source for uncommon and climate-adapted fruit varieties, with a focus on regenerative growing and food justice.
  • 🌾
    Bay Laurel Nursery Reliable mail-order source for bare root temperate fruit trees and berries, including selections grown by Dave Wilson Nursery.
  • 🍎
    Trees of Antiquity Specializes in heirloom fruit trees and berries, with many uncommon and historic varieties available by mail order.
  • 🌧️
    Raintree Nursery Extensive selection of fruit trees, berries, vines, and edible plants suited to home orchards.
  • 🌍
    One Green World Well-known source for unusual, climate-adapted, and hard-to-find edible plants.
  • 🌰
    The Seedman Offers rare and unusual seeds, including tropical and subtropical fruits..
  • 🌾
    Territorial Seed Company Trusted source for vegetable, herb, and fruit seeds selected for Pacific Coast growing conditions.
  • 🌿
    Dave Wilson Nursery Major grower of bare root fruit trees with excellent educational resources on chill hours, ripening times, rootstocks, and backyard orchard culture.
  • 🌴
    Phoenix Tropical Gardens Source for tropical and subtropical plants suited to warm Bay Area microclimates and protected growing areas.
