Directions to the DVC Campus for the Scion Exchange

Public Transportation

BART: The Pleasant Hill BART Station and then the 316 bus to DVC, or the Concord BART Station and then the 314 bus to DVC.

Driving Directions

From Martinez and points North: Take I-680 S to exit 52, Contra Costa Blvd. Continue on Contra Costa Blvd to your destination.

From Hayward: Take I-580 toward Oakland. At the SR-13 exit, take SR-13 toward Berkeley. At the SR-13/SR-24 interchange, take SR-24 toward Walnut Creek, merging onto I-680 N. Take exit 51 for Willow Pass Road toward Taylor Blvd.Take Willow Pass Road onto Sunvalley Blvd. Turn right onto Contra Costa Blvd. Turn left on Golf Club Road, left on Old Quarry Road and locate the Horticulture Building.

From Marin County: Take I-580 eastbound across the Richmond/San Rafael bridge toward Oakland. Take exit 7 from I-580 E. Take Richmond Pkwy and San Pablo Ave to CA-4 E. Follow CA-4 E to I-680 S. Follow I-680 S to exit 52. Turn left onto Contra Costa Blvd. Turn right onto Chilpancingo Pkwy, then left onto Old Quarry Rd.

From San Francisco: Take I-80 east across the Bay Bridge and continue on I-580. Exit on the right to CA-24 toward Walnut Creek. Follow CA-24 and I-680 to Willow Pass Rd (exit 51).Take Sunvalley Blvd, Contra Costa Blvd and then Golf Club Rd to Old Quarry Rd and the campus.
