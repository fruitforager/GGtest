Grafting Tools, Pruners & Orchard Equipment

The tools serious Bay Area growers actually use — from a Berkeley Japanese tool shop to Swiss-made pruners built for a lifetime.

★ Bay Area Destination Berkeley
Hida Tool & Hardware
📍 1333 San Pablo Ave, Berkeley · hidatool.com · (510) 524-3700 · Mon–Sat 10–5

Founded 1982. The Bay Area's destination for authentic Japanese hand tools — forged carbon steel pruners, grafting knives, folding saws, and bonsai tools made using techniques descended from samurai sword-smithing. Edge retention on their carbon steel has no American retail equivalent. Professional sharpening offered (drop Mon, pick up Wed). Customers include Merritt College's horticulture program and serious CRFG members across the chapter.

Visit site
Industry Standard Available Widely
Felco Pruners
📍 Swiss-made · america.felco.com

The benchmark for professional bypass pruners since 1948. Hardened steel blades, every component individually replaceable — a well-maintained Felco lasts decades. The Felco 2 is the classic; the 7 and 8 rotate to reduce wrist strain. Buy once.

Find a reseller
Ships Nationwide
A.M. Leonard
📍 Piqua, OH · amleo.com

Professional orchard-grade supplier. Full Felco line including replacement parts, plus grafting knives, budding strips, grafting tape, plant labels, and orchard ladders. The go-to for professional supplies unavailable locally.

Visit site
Available Widely
Zenport Industries
📍 zenportindustries.com

Value-priced grafting knives, budding blades, grafting tape, and pruners. A sensible starting point for members new to grafting before committing to premium Japanese tools.

Soil, Compost & Orchard Inputs

Bulk materials, organic amendments, and biological inputs for building the kind of soil fruit trees reward over decades.

★ Bay Area Institution San Carlos
Lyngso Garden Materials
📍 345 Shoreway Rd, San Carlos · lyngsogarden.com · Mon–Sat 7–4

Family-run, employee-owned since 1953. Renowned for their organic soil line: actively aerated compost tea, specialty composts, humus blends, and mulches. Staff trained in soil biology. Bulk pickup or delivery. Runs on 100% renewable energy. The Peninsula grower's first call for serious soil work.

Visit site
North Bay Organic Since 1980
Harmony Farm Supply & Nursery
📍 Sebastopol & Petaluma · harmonyfarm.com

Also featured under nurseries. Their amendment side covers organic fertilizers, cover crop seed, beneficial insects, worm bins, compost bins, and ecological pest management. Irrigation design services on site. One of the most complete organic orchard resources in Northern California.

Visit site
Multiple Bay Area
American Soil & Stone
📍 Multiple Bay Area locations · americansoilandstone.com

Bulk landscape and garden materials at multiple Bay Area yards. The practical source for large volumes of compost, topsoil, and amendments when establishing new orchard beds — where small-bag purchasing stops making sense.

California-based drip irrigation specialist since 1991, with a store and demonstration garden in Willits. Orchard-specific kits sized for 20- or 40-tree plantings, complete with regulators, timers, and filters. Staff take questions seriously and can walk through custom setups.

Mail-Order Fruit & Nut Trees

California-based and genuinely superior national sources — chosen for breadth, reputation, and fit with Bay Area growing conditions. Most ship bare-root January through March.

★ Citrus Authority Ships Nationwide
Four Winds Growers
📍 Watsonville, CA (also Fremont & Winters) · fourwindsgrowers.com

Fourth-generation family citrus nursery since 1948. California's premier semi-dwarf citrus source — 60+ varieties plus avocados, blueberries, and olives. Retail pickup in Fremont and Watsonville. Flora Grubb and other respected Bay Area nurseries carry their stock.

Visit site
Chapter Favorite Certified Organic Ships Jan–Apr
Trees of Antiquity
📍 Paso Robles, CA · treesofantiquity.com

Formerly Sonoma Antique Apple Nursery — 40+ years growing certified organic heirloom fruit trees. 400+ varieties including an unmatched heirloom apple selection, plus stone fruit, pluots, and mulberries. The place for varieties that simply don't exist in garden centers.

Visit site
Ships Jan–Mar Also Retail Pickup
Bay Laurel Nursery
📍 Atascadero, CA · baylaurelnursery.com

375+ varieties of bare-root trees, shrubs, and vines. Among the clearest chill-hour and rootstock information of any nursery online. Orders open in September; shipping January through early March. Strong for Asian pears, quinces, and unusual stone fruit.

Visit site
★ Grafters' Source USDA Organic Ships Nationwide
Fruitwood Nursery
📍 Orleans, CA · fruitwoodnursery.com

The former Rolling River Nursery team, now specializing in propagation material. 251 apple varieties, 63 figs, 69 persimmons, 59 pomegranates — as scionwood, cuttings, rootstocks, and liners. Consistently recommended by CRFG chapters for members serious about grafting.

Visit site
Ships Nationwide
One Green World
📍 Molalla, OR · onegreenworld.com

Exceptional breadth of unusual edibles — rare berries, uncommon stone fruit, cold-hardy subtropicals, and mulberries rarely found locally. Good growing notes and sensible recommendations for Northern California climates.

Visit site
Resources Only
Dave Wilson Nursery — Retail Finder
📍 Hickman, CA (wholesale only) · davewilson.com

The largest grower of deciduous fruit trees in the US — wholesale only. Use their website's retail locator to find local DWN stock, and their Backyard Orchard Culture resources and variety database for chill-hour guidance.

Seeds & Companion Edibles

For members expanding into seeds, berries, and edible understory plants that support and complement a home orchard.

Ships Nationwide Open-Pollinated
Territorial Seed Company
📍 Cottage Grove, OR · territorialseed.com

Pacific Coast seed house with strong open-pollinated and heirloom varieties suited to cool-summer climates. Good fit for coastal Bay Area growers. Cover crops, companion plants, berries, and edible perennials alongside their vegetable seed catalog.

Visit site
Ships Nationwide Certified Organic
Strictly Medicinal Seeds
📍 Williams, OR · strictlymedicinalseeds.com

Unusual edibles, medicinal herbs, and rare plants as seed or starts — many unavailable through conventional sources. Useful for members exploring edible perennials, unusual berries, and companion planting beyond the standard nursery palette. Their catalog rewards browsing.
