Golden Gate Chapter Meetings

Meetings are free and open to the public. You don't need to be a CRFG member to attend — just bring your curiosity and enthusiasm for fruit.

Meetings may include an expert speaker, fresh fruit tastings from members' gardens, members only orchard tours and field trips, plant and scion sharing, and great conversation with local growers.

About Our Meetings 🍑

The Golden Gate Chapter hosts gatherings around the Bay Area to hear expert talks, taste members' fruit, and trade plants, scions, and seeds.

Membership adds benefits like garden tours, the Fruit Gardener magazine, and our Festival of Fruit state conference.
