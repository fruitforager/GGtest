Our Favorite Fruity Resources

Recommended websites, research tools, and sustainable gardening resources for Golden Gate Chapter members.

🍊 Fruit Growing & Research
🍊
California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG)
Discover rare fruit-growing techniques, browse expert articles and educational resources, and connect with a community passionate about cultivating unusual and rewarding fruit varieties.
🌎
North American Fruit Explorers (NAFEX)
Explore exceptional fruit and nut varieties, exchange ideas with fellow enthusiasts, and uncover growing knowledge focused on uncommon edible plants.
🏡
Home Orchard Society
Gain practical advice for backyard orchards, improve pruning skills, and navigate fruit tree care with accessible guidance for growers of all experience levels.
🔬
UC Fruit & Nut Research Center
Access university-backed research, examine science-based growing information, and stay informed about fruit and nut production, varieties, and orchard management.
🌿
Pawpaw Research Program
Investigate pawpaw cultivars, follow current research and breeding efforts, and deepen your understanding of one of North America's most unique native fruits.
🌸
Passiflora UK
Browse detailed information on passionfruit and ornamental Passiflora species, identify new varieties, and expand your knowledge of cultivation and care.
🍎
Pomiferous
Search thousands of apple varieties, compare harvest timing and flavor characteristics, and trace the diversity and history of apples worldwide.
🌱 Orchard Care & Pruning
✂️
Summer Pruning Guide (PDF)
Master seasonal pruning techniques, understand proper timing, and strengthen fruit tree health and productivity throughout the growing season.
🍐 Fruit Sharing & Community Harvesting
🍐
Village Harvest
Share excess fruit, volunteer for community harvests, and support local food access through one of the Bay Area's longest-running gleaning organizations.
🍊
Oakland Gleaners
Donate surplus backyard fruit, participate in neighborhood harvests, and help connect fresh produce with East Bay food distribution efforts.
🌿
Climate Roots Tree Gleaning
Volunteer to harvest backyard fruit, support local food security, and help redirect excess produce to community organizations.
🌼 Sustainable Gardening & Urban Agriculture
🌱
Bay-Friendly Gardening
Build healthier soil, improve mulch and compost practices, and adopt sustainable landscaping techniques suited to Bay Area gardens.
🥕
Foodwise
Connect with local farms, attend educational programs, and strengthen your understanding of seasonal food and sustainable agriculture.
🍋
Just One Tree / Urban Sprouts
Support urban food resilience, register backyard citrus trees, and help strengthen San Francisco's community fruit network.
♻️ Compost, Mulch & Soil Resources
♻️
StopWaste Free Compost & Mulch
Locate compost giveaways, access mulch resources, and improve garden soil through Alameda County sustainability programs.
🚛
ChipDrop
Request free arborist wood chips, mulch fruit trees, and conserve soil moisture while improving long-term soil health.
