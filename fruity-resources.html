Our Favorite Fruity Resources

Recommended websites, research tools, and sustainable gardening resources for Golden Gate Chapter members.

🍊
California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG)
The main CRFG website with articles and resources for rare fruit growers.
🌎
North American Fruit Explorers (NAFEX)
Finding, growing, and sharing exceptional fruit and nut varieties.
🏡
Home Orchard Society
Practical advice for backyard orchardists, including pruning and fruit tree care.
🔬
UC Fruit & Nut Research Center
Science-based information and research from the University of California.
🌿
Pawpaw Research Program
KSU's leading source for pawpaw research, cultivars, and growing information.
🌸
Passiflora UK
Information about passionfruit and ornamental Passiflora species.
🍎
Pomiferous
Explore thousands of apple varieties, harvest timing, and characteristics.

Summer Pruning Guide (PDF)
Timing and techniques for pruning fruit trees during the growing season.

StopWaste
Alameda County's resource for composting and sustainable gardening practices.
🚛
ChipDrop
Connecting gardeners with arborists for free wood chips for mulching.
