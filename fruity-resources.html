Favorite Fruit-Growing Resources

Recommended websites, research tools, and sustainable gardening resources for Golden Gate Chapter members.

🍊
California Rare Fruit Growers (CRFG)
The main CRFG website with articles and resources for rare fruit growers.
🌎
North American Fruit Explorers (NAFEX)
A longtime organization for people passionate about finding, growing, and sharing exceptional fruit and nut varieties.
🏡
Home Orchard Society
Excellent practical advice for backyard orchardists, including pruning, espalier, and fruit tree care.
🔬
UC Fruit & Nut Research Center
Science-based information and research from the University of California.
🌿
Pawpaw Research Program
Kentucky State University's leading source for pawpaw research, cultivars, and growing information.
🌸
Passiflora UK
Detailed information about passionfruit and ornamental Passiflora species from enthusiasts and collectors.
🍎
Pomiferous
Explore thousands of apple varieties, harvest timing, pollination groups, and growing characteristics.
✂️
Summer Pruning Guide (PDF)
A practical reference for timing and techniques when pruning fruit trees during the growing season.
♻️
StopWaste
Alameda County's resource for composting, sheet mulching, and sustainable gardening practices perfect for local orchards.
🚛
ChipDrop
A service that connects gardeners with local arborists to receive free wood chips for mulching and soil building.
