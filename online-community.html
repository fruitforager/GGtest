Join Our Fruity Conversation

The Golden Gate Chapter online community is where members stay in touch between meetings — asking questions, sharing harvests, and trading notes with Bay Area fruit growers year-round.

Post what's ripening in your garden, troubleshoot problems with people who grow the same plants, and build on the knowledge of growers who've been at it for decades.

💬 How to Connect Between Meetings

Membership requests are reviewed to keep the group active and spam-free. Once approved, tap into local growing knowledge, get answers fast, and contribute your own experience to the mix.

🌿

Local growing tips

Dig into what works across Bay Area microclimates — from foggy coastal strips to inland heat pockets — and pick up hard-won adaptations from members who've trialed the same varieties.

💬

Member questions

Bring your toughest questions about fruit trees, pests, pruning, and timing — then compare notes with growers who've wrestled with the exact same problems in similar conditions.

📷

Share photos

Capture blossoms, harvests, and mystery ailments and post them for the group — get rapid identification help and let others see what's actually happening in real Bay Area gardens right now.

How it works

  1. Introduce yourself briefly — tell us what you grow, what you're hoping to learn, or what corner of the Bay Area you're gardening in.
  2. Complete the signup form and confirm your email address so we know you're a real person with a genuine interest in fruit growing.
  3. Reach out to us directly at golden_gate@crfg.org if anything gets stuck or you don't hear back within a few days.
