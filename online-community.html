Online community

Connect Between Meetings

Stay connected with Golden Gate Chapter members and Bay Area fruit growers. Share experiences, ask questions, and continue conversations between meetings.

Tell your story, compare notes on what is thriving, and learn from the successes and surprises of other local growers.

💬 Join the Conversation

Membership requests are reviewed to keep the community active and spam-free. Once you join, you can ask questions, share photos, and learn from other local growers.

🌿

Local growing tips

Learn what works in Bay Area microclimates, from foggy coast to inland heat, and hear how others adapt their orchards over time.

💬

Member questions

Ask about fruit trees, varieties, pests, pruning, and seasonal timing, and compare your results with growers who have tried the same plants.

📷

Share photos

Post blossoms, harvests, and problem plants so others can help identify and advise, and so everyone can see what is happening in real gardens.
